Hello, my name is Christopher Shane Brown. I know you are wondering if I go by Christopher or Chris, but I actually go by Shane. On top of that, I didn't really like what I was doing, so it was time for a career change. I did always like programming and computers in school, so I started looking at different IT certification programs, but did not feel like any of them suited me. The more I looked into different options the more I felt drawn to full stack web development. It involves programming and computers and is in high demand and should be for the foreseeable future.
